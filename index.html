About FarmAssist

FarmAssist is a cutting-edge platform designed to empower farmers across India with real-time climate data, current temperatures, humidity levels, and tailored crop recommendations. By integrating advanced technologies and data from NASA and its esteemed Space Agency Partners, we bridge the gap between space-based observations and on-ground agricultural practices.

Our Mission

To enhance agricultural productivity and sustainability by providing farmers with actionable insights derived from satellite data and advanced analytics.

Technologies We Utilize

  • NASA EOSDIS: For comprehensive Earth observation data.
  • European Space Agency Copernicus: For high-resolution satellite imagery.
  • Canadian Space Agency RADARSAT: For soil moisture and land use analysis.
  • Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency ALOS: For topographical mapping.
  • Chart.js: For interactive data visualization.
  • Django & Django REST Framework: For robust backend development.
